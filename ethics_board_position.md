# Position
Cautious; insists on impact assessments, transparency, and safeguards for fundamental rights
## Interests:
- Ensure compliance with ethical principles (fairness, non-discrimination, human oversight, explainability)
- Prevent the normalization of high-risk algorithmic decision-making in sensitive areas like citizenship
## Tensions:
- Between **ethical constraints** and the administration’s **desire for rapid deployment** and **political visibility**
- Between **formal recommendations** and the administration’s actual **power to ignore or water them down**
