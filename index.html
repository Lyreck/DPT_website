ai trade union vision.

We are the first trade union of the ministry of interior and we advocate for the rights of civil servants within all branches: immigration, visa, police, fines, civil protection.

what is at stake?

Social dialogue is a pillar of Lydorian democracy. We fight so that workers are not just consulted, they are heard and taken their positions are taken into account. We protect jobs, working conditions, and professional autonomy of civil servants in the ministry.

We believe this dialogue is critical to ensure puiblic administration remains a good place for civil servants and a service to all citizens, at the foundation of Lydoria's rule of law.

our top three priorities

1. No replacement of human expertise: Ensure that AI supports decision-making rather than replace human judgment.

2. Force a genuine social dialogue: Civil servants can shape the project, not just approve it without any discussion.

3. Be a force of proposition: We criticize, we denounce, but we also propose alternatives that benefit civil servants, the administration, and citizens.
